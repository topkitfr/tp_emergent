<product_requirements>
The TopKit application functions as a Discogs-like collaborative platform for football jersey collectors, focusing on jersey display, image handling, and personal collection management. Initial requirements included UI/UX consistency, fixing homepage elements, renaming Catalogue to Database and Community DB to Contributions, and resolving core bugs in contribution forms and personal details forms. A key change involved removing le vestiaire et le kit store functionality, streamlining to DATABASE, KIT AREA, and CONTRIBUTION pages.

The product underwent a major architectural revamp to simplify kits into two types:
1.  **Master Kit:** An official, shared reference for each jersey design, containing standard information (club, season, type, brand, photos).
2.  **My Collection:** A user's personal copy of a Master Kit, inheriting its details while allowing the user to add personal information (name/number, condition, purchase price).

Recent feature additions and fixes include:
*   A Moderation Dashboard with pagination and Approved/Rejected tabs.
*   Correct authentication flow for Add to Collection buttons, ensuring users are prompted to log in if not authenticated.
*   A Want button for adding jerseys to a wantlist without a form.
*   Master Kit creation forms with specific dropdowns (Club, Season [YEAR-YEAR], Type, Competition, Model, Brand, Sponsors, Gender, Color) and validation for mandatory fields.
*   Master Kit creations are now displayed in Community Contributions and Moderation Dashboard.
</product_requirements>
